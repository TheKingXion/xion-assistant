# Wake Word

Wake word is planned for later phase. Detection should be local when possible.

## Words

- `Oye Xion`
- `Jarvis`
- `Xion`
- User-defined word

## Desktop Limits

Requires local microphone permission and Tauri/native wake-word engine.

## Android Limits

Background listening depends on Android permissions, battery policy and foreground service rules.

## Privacy

No continuous audio is sent to backend. User can disable wake word any time.
